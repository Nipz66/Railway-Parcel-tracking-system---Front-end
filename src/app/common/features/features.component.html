Features of the Railway Parcel Tracking System

The Railway Parcel Tracking System is designed to streamline and enhance the management of parcels transported through the railway network. This system allows for seamless tracking, secure handling, and efficient management of parcels across multiple stations, ensuring that both staff and administrators can manage the parcel flow effectively.

Real-Time Parcel Tracking

Track parcels in real time as they move through various railway stations, with instant updates on the current location and status.

Role-Based Access

Provides separate dashboards for Admins and Staff. Admins can manage users, while Staff can update parcel information and statuses.

Parcel History and Status Updates

View the complete history of each parcel, including time-stamped status updates at every station it passes through.

Station-Based Parcel Transactions

Manage parcel send and receive transactions at each station, ensuring smooth and secure parcel handling across locations.
